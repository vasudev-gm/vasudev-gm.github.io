### Quick Report

Samsung posted a sharp Q1 2026 earnings jump led by memory demand, with chip division operating income reported at 53.7 trillion won and overall operating profit at 57.2 trillion won on 133.9 trillion won in revenue. The advanced-memory demand from AI data-center customers and multi-year supply agreements were the primary drivers of the result.
<!-- more -->

Samsung also warned that the memory shortfall could widen further in 2027 as order visibility remains strong and supply lags expected demand, even as HBM output scales. The company says HBM4 mass-production sales began in February for NVIDIA\'s Vera Rubin platform, while other business lines such as mobile and displays faced margin pressure from rising component costs.
