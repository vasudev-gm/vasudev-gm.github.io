### Quick Report

Microsoft is ending phone-based activation, shifting Windows and Office licensing toward Microsoft account sign-in. The move modernizes verification and aligns with cloud-era policies, though some offline scenarios may require alternative support paths.

The change affects recent Windows 11 and Office builds, consolidating authentication under Microsoft accounts and reducing legacy activation pathways.
