### Quick Report

Microsoft has started removing visible Copilot buttons and branding from several core Windows 11 apps, including Notepad and Snipping Tool, after sustained user pushback around forced AI presence. In the latest app revisions, those integrations are being repositioned with less prominent naming, such as generic **Writing Tools** labels.
<!-- more -->

The change signals a UI and product strategy retreat rather than a full removal of AI-backed functions, since rewrite and summary capabilities can still exist behind the scenes. Even so, the update marks one of Microsoft\'s clearest acknowledgements that "AI everywhere" in basic desktop tools created more friction than value for many users.
