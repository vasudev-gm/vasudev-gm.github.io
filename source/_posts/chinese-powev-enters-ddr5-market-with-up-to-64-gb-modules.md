### Quick Report

POWEV, a Chinese sub-brand of Jiahe Jinwei, has entered the DDR5 market with up to 64 GB modules across UDIMM, SODIMM, and RDIMM form factors. The lineup is split between consumer-style and industrial memory, with options running at 4800 MT/s or 5600 MT/s.<!-- more -->

The launch matters because it adds another domestic DDR5 supplier while the broader memory market remains tight. POWEV says its modules are already shipping to Chinese data centers, and consumer availability is set to follow as the company builds out volume production.
