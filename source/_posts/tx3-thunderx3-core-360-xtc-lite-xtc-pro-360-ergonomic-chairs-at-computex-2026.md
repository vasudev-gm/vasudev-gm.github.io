### Quick Report

TX3 (ThunderX3) displayed three ergonomic chairs at Computex: the Core 360 gaming chair with CORE.RDY 2.0 self-adjusting lumbar support and SYNC5 tilt, plus the XTC Lite and XTC Pro 360 mesh office chairs with multi-axis armrests and adjustable seat depth.<!-- more -->

The Core 360 pairs AIR Tech mesh and memory-foam seating with 5D x 360 armrests and a car seat–inspired headrest; the XTC Pro 360 adds more configurability over the Lite model, targeting long-session comfort for office and studio use.
