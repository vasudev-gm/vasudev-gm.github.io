### Quick Report

Sony has suspended new orders for nearly its full SD and CFexpress memory card portfolio, citing ongoing semiconductor and component shortages that continue to constrain supply. The pause affects distributors and direct Sony Store channels, while retail inventory already in the channel can still sell through until stock is depleted.
<!-- more -->

The impacted lineup includes all listed CFexpress Type A capacities and several Type B SKUs, plus most of Sony\'s SD ranges including TOUGH-branded and V60 cards, with even some V30 models on hold. Sony says it will revisit order resumption based on future supply conditions, but it hasn\'t provided a firm restart date, leaving media professionals and creators to plan around tighter availability.
