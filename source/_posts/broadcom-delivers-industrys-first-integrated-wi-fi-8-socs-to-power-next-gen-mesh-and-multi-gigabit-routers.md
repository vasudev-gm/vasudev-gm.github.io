### Quick Report

Broadcom has announced three integrated Wi-Fi 8 SoCs for consumer networking gear: BCM6772, BCM6774, and BCM6776. The new parts combine the application processor, network engine, Wi-Fi 8 radios, and multi-gigabit Ethernet PHY on one die to simplify design for mesh nodes and high-speed routers.
<!-- more -->

Across the family, Broadcom highlights quad-core CPU resources, integrated 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz radio blocks, and cost and power benefits from on-chip amplifier and digital pre-distortion technology. The company says the BCM677x lineup is currently sampling with early access partners and is aimed at next-generation dual-band and tri-band home networking platforms.
