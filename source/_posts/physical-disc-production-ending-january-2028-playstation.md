### Quick Report

Sony Interactive Entertainment announced that physical disc production for all new games releasing on PlayStation consoles will be discontinued starting January 2028. Following this date, new titles will be available exclusively through PlayStation Store and digital retailers. Games released prior to January 2028 will continue to be available in disc format, and the decision does not affect existing game libraries or backward compatibility with physical media.
<!-- more -->

Sony framed the transition as a natural evolution responding to consumer preferences shifting away from physical media toward digital formats. The company states this aligns with how "most of our community prefers to access and play games today." PlayStation 5 currently offers a disc drive option, but the shift signals that future PlayStation hardware may be digital-only. The move echoes industry-wide trends toward digital distribution and follows similar discussions around media preservation and consumer access, particularly for players who prefer owning physical copies.
