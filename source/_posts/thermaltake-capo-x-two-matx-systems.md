### Quick Report

Thermaltake unveiled CAPO X, a tall tower case engineered to host two equal microATX systems side-by-side, with room for long graphics cards and 360 mm AIO coolers in each chamber.

The case features a pillarless tempered glass panel and supports dual independent liquid-cooling loops and full-sized GPU clearances, making it suitable for dual workstation or streaming+gaming builds.
