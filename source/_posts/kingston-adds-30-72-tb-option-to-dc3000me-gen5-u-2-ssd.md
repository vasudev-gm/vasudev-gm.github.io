### Quick Report

Kingston has launched a 30.72 TB variant of its DC3000ME Gen5 U.2 NVMe SSD, extending the family for denser enterprise and cloud deployments. The drive uses a PCIe 5.0 interface and is positioned for AI, HPC, and large-scale data-center workloads that need high capacity with low-latency access.
<!-- more -->

The company rates the DC3000ME for up to 14 GB/s sequential reads and up to 2.8 million random-read IOPS, while keeping backward compatibility with PCIe 4.0 server platforms. Kingston also emphasizes enterprise safeguards including 3D eTLC NAND, onboard power-loss protection, AES 256-bit encryption, TCG Opal 2.0 SED support, plus a 5-year limited warranty.
