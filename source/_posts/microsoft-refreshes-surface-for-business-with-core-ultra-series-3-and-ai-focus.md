### Quick Report

Microsoft has refreshed its Surface for Business lineup with new Surface Laptop and Surface Pro models powered by Intel Core Ultra Series 3 processors. The new systems are pitched around AI acceleration, stronger security, and tighter integration with the Microsoft management stack.
<!-- more -->

Pricing starts at $1,299.99 for the entry Surface Laptop for Business 13-inch and $1,949.99 for the larger Surface Laptop and Surface Pro models, while an 8 GB configuration arrives later this year. Microsoft also highlighted a removable Gen 4 SSD, Wi-Fi 7, an optional privacy screen, and Snapdragon X2-based models coming later.
