### Quick Report

Moore Threads has announced its upcoming Lushan gaming and Huashan AI GPUs, both based on the new Flower Harbor (Huagang) architecture and set for a 2026 launch. The company claims the Lushan chip will deliver up to 15x performance in AAA games, a 50x boost in ray tracing, 64x improvement in AI calculations, 16x geometric processing, and 4x texturing throughput compared to its previous generation.
<!-- more -->

The new GPUs will support DirectX 12 Ultimate from launch, aiming to close the software gap with established competitors. The Huashan AI GPU features a chiplet design with eight HBM slots and is positioned to compete with NVIDIA\'s Hopper series in AI workloads. Moore Threads\' aggressive performance targets and recent $1.1 billion IPO highlight its ambition to become a major player in both gaming and AI GPU markets.
