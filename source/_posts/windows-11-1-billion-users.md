### Quick Report

Microsoft has announced that Windows 11 has reached 1 billion users worldwide, achieving this milestone in just 1,576 days i.e 130 days faster than Windows 10. The rapid adoption is attributed to the end-of-life of Windows 10, prompting users and enterprises to upgrade. Windows 11, launched in October 2021, has evolved from a Windows 10 reskin to a modernized OS, despite facing criticism for bugs and update issues.
<!-- more -->

Microsoft plans to keep Windows 11 as a long-running version, with no immediate successor in sight. The latest quarterly report shows a 5% year-over-year growth for Windows, though this is slightly below company expectations. The milestone underscores Microsoft\'s dominance in the desktop OS market.
