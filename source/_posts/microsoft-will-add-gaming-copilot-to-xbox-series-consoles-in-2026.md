### Quick Report

Microsoft has confirmed at GDC that Gaming Copilot is planned to reach current generation Xbox Series X|S consoles before the end of 2026, extending a feature already seen on PC and mobile. The assistant is pitched as an in game helper that can offer recommendations, strategy hints, and context aware guidance to get players into gameplay faster.
<!-- more -->

As with earlier rollouts, the feature relies on screenshot and OCR style analysis to generate responses, which keeps privacy concerns in focus for users who do not want gameplay captures processed for model workflows. Microsoft previously offered a settings toggle on supported platforms, but default behavior on consoles has not been fully detailed yet.
