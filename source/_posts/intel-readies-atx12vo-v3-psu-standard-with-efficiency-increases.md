### Quick Report

A new leak suggests Intel is preparing ATX12VO V3 with another round of efficiency-focused changes to its 12-volt-only desktop power standard. The slide set points to a new 8-pin connector path, removal of the standby rail, and new Low Power and High Power operating modes.
<!-- more -->

TechPowerUp reports the leaked data claims up to 29 percent better idle efficiency and up to 12 percent better load efficiency against regular multi-rail ATX designs. The update also appears to add PMBUS support from server ecosystems through four optional motherboard communication pins, enabling richer PSU-to-system telemetry and control if platform vendors adopt the full feature set.
