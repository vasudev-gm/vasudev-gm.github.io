### Quick Report

New sales figures from German retailer Mindfactory show the Radeon RX 9070 XT leading unit share for weeks 9 through 11 of 2026, with AMD taking a majority share at that outlet during the period. The same dataset also points to a sharp volume decline, with total GPU sales reportedly dropping to around one third of more typical levels.
<!-- more -->

In the breakdown highlighted by TPU, the RX 9070 XT and RX 9060 XT hold the top two positions, while the next several ranks are occupied by GeForce models including RTX 5080, RTX 5070 Ti, RTX 5060, RTX 5070, and RTX 5090. The report ties weaker overall demand to persistent pricing pressure and broader component cost stress, while also noting that Mindfactory trends can diverge from global market share patterns.
