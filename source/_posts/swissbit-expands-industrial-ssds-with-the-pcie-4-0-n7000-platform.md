### Quick Report

Swissbit has launched the N7000, a PCIe Gen 4 industrial SSD platform designed for embedded, networking, and edge-AI systems. The family spans more than 30 variants and is built around power efficiency, predictable behavior, and supply-chain control through Swissbit-owned controller and firmware stacks.
<!-- more -->

The lineup covers 240 GB to 3.84 TB in M.2 2242 and M.2 2280 formats, with C-grade and I-grade temperature options, AES-256 encryption, TCG Opal 2.0, crypto erase, and NVMe power-loss protection. Swissbit also previewed pSLC-based N7600 models and the harsher-environment N7001 and N7601 follow-ups.
