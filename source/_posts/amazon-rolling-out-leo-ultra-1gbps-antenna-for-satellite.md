### Quick Report

Amazon is launching its Leo Ultra 1Gbps antenna for satellite connectivity, targeting enterprise customers with high-speed, low-latency internet. The new antenna is now undergoing enterprise testing and is designed to support next-generation satellite broadband services.
<!-- more -->

The Leo Ultra antenna is part of Amazon\'s Project Kuiper, which aims to provide global broadband coverage using a constellation of low Earth orbit (LEO) satellites. With 1Gbps throughput, the antenna is expected to deliver reliable connectivity for demanding business applications, remote operations, and underserved regions. Amazon\'s move intensifies competition in the satellite internet market, challenging established players like SpaceX Starlink.
