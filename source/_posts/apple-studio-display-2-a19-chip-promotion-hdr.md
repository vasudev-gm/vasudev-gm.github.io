### Quick Report

Apple is preparing to launch the Studio Display 2 in 2026, targeting creators and professionals with a major upgrade: a 3nm A19 chip, 120Hz ProMotion, and Mini LED HDR.
<!-- more -->

The new Studio Display 2 will support variable refresh rates up to 120 Hz, a significant jump from the previous 60 Hz limit. HDR support is expected, leveraging Mini LED technology for up to 1,000 nits sustained and 1,600 nits peak brightness, similar to the latest MacBook Pro displays. The display will be powered by Apple\'s latest A19 SoC, replacing the older A13, to handle advanced processing, connectivity, and calibration features. The Studio Display 2 is expected to launch alongside the next-generation Mac Studio with M5 series chips.
