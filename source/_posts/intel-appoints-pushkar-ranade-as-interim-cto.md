### Quick Report

Intel has appointed Pushkar Ranade as interim chief technology officer following the recent departure of Sachin Katti. Ranade, a veteran at Intel with experience from 65 nm node development to 7 nm SoCs, will help shape the company\'s advanced technology strategy and oversee critical emerging technologies.
<!-- more -->

His responsibilities include consolidating and developing areas such as quantum computing, advanced interconnects, and novel materials within the new CTO Office. Ranade will work closely with CEO Lip-Bu Tan to execute Intel\'s vision for innovation and revitalization. The company also announced new appointments in government affairs and marketing to strengthen its leadership team.
