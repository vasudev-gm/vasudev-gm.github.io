### Quick Report

OneXPlayer has launched the ONEXStation mini PC built around AMD\'s Ryzen AI Max+ 395 platform, pairing the chip with Radeon 8060S graphics and up to 128 GB of LPDDR5X on a 256-bit bus. The system enters the market at a $2,999 launch price, with reports indicating a higher $3,599 MSRP target after the introductory window.
<!-- more -->

The compact 186 x 193 x 62 mm chassis includes 13 total ports, dual USB4, 2.5 GbE, Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.4, and a configurable 55 W, 85 W, or 120 W TDP profile with a dedicated turbo mode. Storage starts with a 1 TB PCIe Gen4 M.2 SSD and supports a second M.2 slot for expansion, positioning the device toward mixed gaming, creator, and local AI or remote workload use cases rather than pure value-focused gaming builds.
