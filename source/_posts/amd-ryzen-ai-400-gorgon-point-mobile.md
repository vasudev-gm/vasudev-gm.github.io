### Quick Report

AMD announced Ryzen AI 400 \"Gorgon Point\" mobile processors—a refresh of Strix Point—bringing higher clocks across the CPU and iGPU, improved boost residency, a faster NPU rated up to 60 TOPS, and native LPDDR5X-8533 support on select models. Flagship SKUs pair 4 Zen 5 cores with 8 Zen 5c, RDNA 3.5 graphics up to 16 CUs at 3.10 GHz, and the top NPU configuration.<!-- more -->

The lineup spans Ryzen AI 9 HX down to Ryzen AI 5, with mixes of Zen 5/Zen 5c cores, varying iGPU CUs, and NPU throughput between 50–60 TOPS. AMD\'s ROCm stack enables client AI workloads to leverage the NPU alongside the iGPU and CPU. Notebook availability starts late January 2026 across major OEMs.
