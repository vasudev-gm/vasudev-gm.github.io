### Quick Report

Corsair previewed Benchlab 2, a collaboration with Open Benchtable and ElmorLabs that brings improved telemetry (per-pin 12VHPWR monitoring and buzzer alerts), a six-layer PCB and iCUE integration; the platform is slated to ship in late 2026.<!-- more -->

Benchlab 2 adds per-pin monitoring for problematic 12VHPWR connectors, built-in buzzer alerts, support for two SSDs, physical power/reset buttons and planned integration with Corsair iCUE for live telemetry and alerts.
