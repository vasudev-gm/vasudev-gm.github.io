### Quick Report

Bazzite has begun shipping its Fedora 44-based release, but only desktop images are broadly available for now while handheld variants remain in a slower rollout track. The update brings Gnome 50 and KDE Plasma 6.6 era changes, plus Bazzite-specific additions like newer Bazaar app store bits, improved ISO image security, Elgato 4K capture support, and refreshed KDE login manager behavior.
<!-- more -->

The project says handheld builds are being delayed intentionally due to the size of the transition, with maintainers prioritizing stability for existing users before wider deployment. Bazzite also remains on a 6.19.x kernel branch for this cycle, with the team indicating more handheld news in coming weeks and pointing to future kernel 7 plans that include newer Linux gaming-focused VRAM management work.
