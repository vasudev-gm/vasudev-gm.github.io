### Quick Report

YMTC has launched the PC550, its first PCIe 5.0 x4 NVMe 2.0 client SSD line, in both M.2 2242 and 2280 form factors for OEM and notebook-focused deployments. Capacities span 512 GB, 1 TB, and 2 TB, with peak sequential throughput rated up to 10,500 MB/s read and 10,000 MB/s write on higher-capacity models.
<!-- more -->

The PC550 uses YMTC\'s Xtacking 4.0 NAND and a four-channel design, which the company positions as a balance between Gen5 performance, thermals, and power draw. While the headline speed trails the fastest premium Gen5 drives, the platform could still become relevant in cost-sensitive client systems as global memory and storage supply pressures continue.
