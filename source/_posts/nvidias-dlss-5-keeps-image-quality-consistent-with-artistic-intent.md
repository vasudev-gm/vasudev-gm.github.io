### Quick Report

NVIDIA has shared additional DLSS 5 details and says the new stack is designed to improve realism without drifting away from how developers intended each scene to look. The company describes a frame process anchored by game color and motion vectors, then enhanced with neural lighting and material reconstruction to keep output stable over time.
<!-- more -->

NVIDIA also says DLSS 5 is being tuned for broad GeForce RTX deployment ahead of a planned fall 2026 rollout, even though early demonstrations used two GeForce RTX 5090 GPUs to split rendering and model execution. The launch list shared so far includes several major titles, with more integrations expected before release.
