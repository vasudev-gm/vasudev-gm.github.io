### Quick Report

Apple has launched the iPhone 17e, emphasizing both advanced technology and environmental responsibility. The device is made with 30% recycled content, including 85% recycled aluminium in the enclosure and 100% recycled cobalt, lithium, copper, gold, tin, rare earth elements, and tungsten in various components.<!-- more -->

The iPhone 17e overview also highlights a Super Retina XDR display, Ceramic Shield front protection, and USB-C connectivity. Apple positions the device around everyday performance, safety, and efficiency, while keeping support for Apple Intelligence features and key services.

The iPhone 17e is IP68 rated for water and dust resistance, supports Apple Intelligence (beta), and uses 100% fibre-based packaging. Apple aims for carbon neutrality by 2030, with 55% of manufacturing electricity sourced from renewables. The phone also supports MagSafe wireless charging, eSIM, and features a crash detection system. For more details, see the official Apple specs and environmental report.
