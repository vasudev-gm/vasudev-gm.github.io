### Quick Report

Apacer launched GraTherX, a thermal solution that reduces DDR5 module temperatures by up to 23.4°C in device-level tests, targeting improved stability and overclocking headroom.
<!-- more -->

Apacer reports the solution combines revised heat spreader geometry and improved thermal interface materials; testing on select DDR5 modules produced the temperature reductions under specified workloads.

The company positions GraTherX for enthusiasts and systems where sustained memory performance and thermal headroom are critical.
