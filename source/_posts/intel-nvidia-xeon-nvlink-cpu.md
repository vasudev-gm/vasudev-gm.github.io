### Quick Report

Intel CEO Lip-Bu Tan has confirmed that Intel and NVIDIA are co-developing a custom Xeon CPU with full NVLink integration. This collaboration aims to deliver best-in-class x86 performance for AI host nodes, directly connecting Xeon CPUs to NVIDIA GPUs for high-bandwidth, low-latency data transfer.
<!-- more -->

The announcement was made during Intel\'s Q4 2025 earnings call, with details still scarce. The project is expected to leverage future Xeon technologies, possibly Diamond Rapids or Coral Rapids, and could launch as early as 2027. This move signals a major shift in AI server architecture, strengthening the Intel-NVIDIA partnership in the datacenter space.
