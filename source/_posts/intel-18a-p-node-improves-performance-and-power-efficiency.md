### Quick Report

Intel says its 18A-P process can deliver over 9% higher performance at the same power or over 18% lower power at the same performance, based on data prepared for VLSI 2026. The company positions 18A-P as a performance-enhanced variant of 18A that keeps similar density while improving operating characteristics.
<!-- more -->

Beyond raw perf-per-watt metrics, Intel is highlighting tighter skew corners, additional logic VT options, improved interconnect behavior, and lower thermal resistance for better heat conduction. The result is a node that should offer more predictable parametric yield behavior and a stronger option set for customers who want 18A-class density with better efficiency and frequency headroom.
