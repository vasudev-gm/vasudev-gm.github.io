### Quick Report

NVIDIA has formally launched DLSS 4.5\'s Dynamic Multi Frame Generation and the new 6x mode through an updated NVIDIA app beta, enabling up to five AI-generated frames per traditionally rendered frame in supported titles. With the right setup, NVIDIA positions this as a major uplift path for frame rate targets, including very high-refresh scenarios.
<!-- more -->

Dynamic MFG is designed to auto-select a practical multiplier based on refresh rate and incoming frame throughput, so the system can scale from lower to higher frame-gen levels as scene load changes. Access requires enabling beta and experimental features in the NVIDIA app plus GeForce Game Ready Driver 595.79 WHQL or newer, and the headline Dynamic/6x capabilities remain tied to RTX 50-series hardware due to Blackwell-specific flip-metering support.
