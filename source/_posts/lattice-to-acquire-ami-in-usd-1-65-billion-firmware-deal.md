### Quick Report

Lattice Semiconductor has agreed to buy American Megatrends Inc. (AMI) for $1.65 billion in cash and stock, a deal that links a major FPGA supplier with one of the dominant UEFI/BIOS platform vendors. The transaction follows recent consolidation in firmware tooling and leaves Insyde as the main independent player in the top-tier PC firmware ecosystem.
<!-- more -->

Lattice says the move supports its broader companion-chip and platform strategy, though the exact product-level integration path is still unclear. Given AMI\'s footprint across mainstream motherboard and system vendors, the acquisition could influence firmware roadmaps well beyond traditional embedded and FPGA-adjacent segments once approvals and integration progress.
