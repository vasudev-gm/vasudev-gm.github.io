### Quick Report

AMD is planning to launch the mid-range Navi 44 and 48 GPUs in March 2025 with initial product announcement at CES 2025 on Jan 6 by AMD CEO Lisa Su.
<!-- more -->

Official First Party RX 9070 boards are expected to be launched late January 2025 while AIB models will ramp up production in late February 2025 after Chinese Spring Festival. AMD RDNA 4 is targeting mid-range gaming and AI workloads t compelling price-point to gain market share from NVIDIA.
