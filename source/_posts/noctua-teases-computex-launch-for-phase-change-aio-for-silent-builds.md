### Quick Report

Noctua has teased a Computex 2026 launch for its long-previewed thermosiphon AIO concept, signaling a pump-free phase-change design aimed at reducing the low-frequency hum often associated with standard liquid coolers. The company used the phrase Vaporisation enhanced in its social teaser and pointed directly to the show timeline.
<!-- more -->

This design was first shown publicly as a prototype at Computex 2024 and is framed as a low-maintenance approach because it avoids moving pump hardware. Noctua also repeated that its partnership with Asetek for a conventional AIO remains active, suggesting both cooler lines may be introduced side by side at Computex 2026.
