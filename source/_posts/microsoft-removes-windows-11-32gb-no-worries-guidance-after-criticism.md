### Quick Report

Microsoft has pulled its earlier Windows 11 blog guidance that described 32 GB RAM as a **no worries** setup for gaming and multitasking, following strong community backlash. TPU reports the original page now redirects to broader Windows Learning Center content instead of preserving the recommendation in place.
<!-- more -->

The controversy arrived during a period of unusually high memory prices and ongoing criticism of Windows 11 resource use, which amplified the negative response. While Microsoft has separately promised performance and optimization improvements, this rollback signals a communications reset as users continue to scrutinize practical RAM requirements for mainstream gaming PCs.
