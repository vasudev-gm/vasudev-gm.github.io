### Quick Report

OneOdio has launched the Studio Max 2, a flagship wireless headphone model developed with DJ and producer KSHMR. The headset uses the company\'s RapidWill+ 3.0 system, 2.4 GHz wireless, and a claimed 9 ms latency target aimed at pro audio and live performance use.
<!-- more -->

The Studio Max 2 also includes a 45 mm driver, LDAC, Bluetooth 6.0, and up to 120 hours of battery life, with pricing set at $189.99 for the standard model. A limited signed edition is capped at 1,000 units and sells for $199.99, which makes the launch feel closer to a niche studio product than a mass-market wireless headset.
