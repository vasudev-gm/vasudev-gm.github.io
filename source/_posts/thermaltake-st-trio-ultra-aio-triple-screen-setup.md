### Quick Report

Thermaltake showed the ST Trio Ultra, a 360 mm AIO CLC whose pump-block integrates three fold-out 6-inch 1280×720 displays to create a tablet-sized display head; a Pro variant replaces the trio with a single 6-inch OLED (2160×1080).
<!-- more -->

The displays are magnetically detachable, controllable via TT RGB Plus 3.0 and ARGB Sync, and the cooler is offered in a white "Snow" variant; the design focuses on novelty and streaming/monitoring use cases.
