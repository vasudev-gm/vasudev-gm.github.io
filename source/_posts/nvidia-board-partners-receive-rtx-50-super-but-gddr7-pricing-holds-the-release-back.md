### Quick Report

NVIDIA\'s GeForce RTX 50 SUPER graphics cards reportedly reached board partners, but the launch is still being held back by shortages of 3 GB GDDR7 memory modules. The delay suggests that pricing and availability of the memory itself may matter more than the silicon packaging for the time being.
<!-- more -->

The reported issue is that 3 GB GDDR7 modules are also being used in NVIDIA\'s AI GPU lineup, including RTX PRO 6000 products, so the company may be prioritizing higher-margin data-center and professional products over gaming cards. That would leave the standard GeForce RTX 50 family to continue using more readily available 2 GB GDDR7 modules for now.
