### Quick Report

Chuwi has unveiled the UniBook, a new 14-inch laptop built around Intel Wildcat Lake Core 3 304 processor. The company is aiming it at students and young professionals, with an estimated starting price of $449 and a claimed 15 to 20 hours of battery life.<!-- more -->

Its configuration includes an FHD 1920 x 1200 IPS panel, 8 GB of LPDDR5X memory, a 256 GB PCIe 3.0 SSD, Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, a 53.38 Wh battery, and Windows 11 Pro. It also carries a practical port mix, including dual USB-C, HDMI 2.0, USB-A, Gigabit Ethernet, and a TF card slot.
