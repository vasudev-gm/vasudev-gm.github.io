### Quick Report

Texas Instruments has announced a definitive agreement to acquire Silicon Labs for $7.5 billion in cash. The deal will combine TI\'s analog and embedded processing strengths with Silicon Labs\' wireless connectivity portfolio, creating a global leader in embedded wireless solutions.
<!-- more -->

The acquisition aims to accelerate innovation, leverage TI\'s internal manufacturing (including 300mm fabs), and deepen customer engagement. Silicon Labs\' manufacturing will be reshored from external foundries, and the combined company expects $450 million in annual synergies within three years. The transaction is set to close in the first half of 2027, pending regulatory and shareholder approvals.
