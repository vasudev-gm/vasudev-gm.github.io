### Quick Report

Apple and Intel Foundry Services have reached a preliminary agreement that could bring Apple Silicon production onto Intel\'s U.S. foundry nodes. The report says Apple is evaluating Intel 18A, which would reduce its near-total reliance on TSMC if the arrangement moves beyond the current planning stage.
<!-- more -->

The move would also reinforce Intel\'s push to become a strategic domestic foundry for major customers. Intel already runs 18A production in Oregon and is building out Ohio capacity, so Apple\'s interest could be another sign that U.S.-based chip manufacturing is becoming a more serious option for high-volume mobile and PC silicon.
