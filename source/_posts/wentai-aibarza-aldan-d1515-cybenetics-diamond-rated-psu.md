### Quick Report

Wentai unveiled the AiBARZA Aldan-D1515, a 1300 W PSU and the first production unit to earn Cybenetics Diamond certification (≥95% continuous switching efficiency), offering fanless operation up to 40% load and four 12V-2x6 connectors for high-end builds.
<!-- more -->

The PSU is also Cybenetics Acoustics A++ rated, includes four 8-pin EPS connectors for workstation use, and carries a suggested price of $932 (10 units were sold at Computex for $745 each). It\'s positioned for enterprise and high-end workstation markets.
