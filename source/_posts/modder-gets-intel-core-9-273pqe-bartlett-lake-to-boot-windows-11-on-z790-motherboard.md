### Quick Report

A hardware modder has reportedly booted Intel\'s Core 9 273PQE Bartlett Lake processor into Windows 11 on a standard Z790 motherboard after firmware-level modifications. This is notable because Bartlett Lake edge and embedded SKUs were not intended for mainstream desktop board support.

The demonstration suggests the major blocker was platform firmware initialization and microcode handling rather than pure socket incompatibility, since Bartlett Lake shares LGA1700 physical fitment. While this proof of concept expands what enthusiasts may attempt on consumer boards, it remains experimental and unsupported, with stability and long-term update behavior still uncertain.
