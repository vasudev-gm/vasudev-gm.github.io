### Quick Report

The Xbox Player Voice forum has quickly surfaced three dominant requests from players: more exclusives, free online multiplayer, and expanded backward compatibility support. The top entries gathered well over 15,000 votes each, with exclusives passing 20,000 votes at the time of reporting.
<!-- more -->

The free multiplayer request focuses on removing the need for paid Game Pass Essential access for non-free online games, while backward compatibility requests ask for wider support for older disc libraries on current Xbox hardware. Microsoft had not published an official response to the top requests in the thread, but the voting trends show clear pressure points from core console users.
