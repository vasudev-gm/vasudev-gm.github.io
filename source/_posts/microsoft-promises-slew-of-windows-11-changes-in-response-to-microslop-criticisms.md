### Quick Report

Microsoft has outlined a broader Windows 11 quality plan after sustained criticism over stability and usability regressions, promising multiple interface and policy changes through upcoming Insider builds. The roadmap includes more taskbar placement options, reduced Copilot integration in several built-in apps, and additional user control over update timing and restart behavior.
<!-- more -->

The company also says it is targeting better File Explorer responsiveness and smoother everyday navigation, alongside a revamped Feedback Hub intended to reduce friction in user reporting. Early changes are expected to appear across March and April Insider channels, with wider rollout timing depending on test feedback.
