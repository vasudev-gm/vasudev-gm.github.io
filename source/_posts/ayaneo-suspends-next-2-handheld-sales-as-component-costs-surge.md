### Quick Report

Ayaneo says it has suspended sales and preorders of its Next 2 Windows handheld after a sharp rise in component pricing made the product financially unsustainable at announced price points. The company specifically pointed to storage, memory, and processor costs that climbed far above earlier quotes used during launch planning.<!-- more -->

According to the update cited by TechPowerUp, even high-end configurations were no longer sufficient to offset losses as supplier pricing moved after the Chinese New Year period. Ayaneo framed the move as a difficult but necessary pause, highlighting the disadvantage smaller hardware vendors face when negotiating long-term parts contracts compared with larger OEMs that can absorb thinner margins for longer periods.
