### Quick Report

Apple is reportedly stepping away from a full next-generation Vision Pro effort after the M5 refresh failed to revive demand, according to a MacRumors-sourced report highlighted by TPU. The headset line sold around 600,000 units after launching at $3,499, and the updated model appears to have faced similar resistance from mainstream buyers.
<!-- more -->

The report points to core friction around price and ergonomics, with the current Vision Pro design weighing roughly 750-800 g and drawing complaints about front-heavy pressure. Apple is now said to be prioritizing a lighter smart-glasses direction with fewer onboard compute demands, potentially using a more incremental path before returning to a fuller immersive platform.
