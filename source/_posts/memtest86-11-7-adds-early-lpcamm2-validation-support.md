### Quick Report

PassMark has released MemTest86 v11.7 (Build 1000) with preliminary LPCAMM2 support, including early faulty-die decoding and expanded module reporting on supported Intel Meteor Lake and Arrow Lake systems. The update also improves slot and form-factor visibility in reports, reflecting rising adoption of LPCAMM2 across new laptop designs.
<!-- more -->

Beyond LPCAMM2 basics, the release adds broader platform coverage for Intel Bartlett Lake, Raptor Lake variants, and Arrow Lake variants, plus several timing, ECC, and decoding fixes. This arrives as CAMM-class memory modules gain momentum, making standardized validation tooling increasingly important for OEM bring-up and field diagnostics.
