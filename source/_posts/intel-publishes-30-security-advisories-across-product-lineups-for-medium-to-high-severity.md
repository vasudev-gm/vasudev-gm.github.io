### Quick Report

Intel has published 30 new security advisories spanning its product portfolio, with vulnerabilities ranging from medium to high severity. The advisories cover everything from 12th to 14th Gen Core and Core Ultra CPUs, multiple Xeon Scalable families, and a broad set of drivers and utilities. Notable advisories include fixes for Intel PROSet/Wireless WiFi, Arc and Iris Xe graphics drivers, Rapid Storage Technology, Killer networking software, and server configuration utilities.
<!-- more -->

The new microcode update (version 20251111) brings functional fixes for recent CPUs, while the security advisories address privilege escalation, stability, and connectivity issues. Users are strongly encouraged to update their drivers and utilities—such as WiFi, graphics, and storage drivers—to the latest versions to reduce exposure to potential exploits. For a full list of advisories and affected products, visit the Intel Security Center.
