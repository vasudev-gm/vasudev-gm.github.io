### Quick Report

TSMC approved another $20 billion for its Arizona Fab 21 site, pushing the Phoenix expansion deeper into one of the largest semiconductor projects in the United States. The new capital covers more land, more EUV equipment, and additional manufacturing tools on top of the already massive investment in the region.
<!-- more -->

The company said the Arizona operation generated about $514 million in profit in its first year, but the buildout still faces hard constraints. Water availability and a shortage of skilled labor remain the main pressure points, so the expansion will continue to move slowly even with the added funding.
