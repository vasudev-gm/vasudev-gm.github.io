### Quick Report

Kioxia has introduced the EXCERIA PRO G2 SDXC UHS-II memory card series, designed for 8K video recording and high-speed continuous shooting. The lineup includes V90 and V60 models: the V90 targets professionals with up to 512GB capacity and read/write speeds of 310/300 MB/s, while the V60 offers a more affordable option with up to 300/250 MB/s.
<!-- more -->

These cards are aimed at photographers, filmmakers, and content creators needing fast, reliable storage for demanding workflows. The new series will be available from March 2026.
