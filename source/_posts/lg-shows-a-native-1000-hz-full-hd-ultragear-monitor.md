### Quick Report

LG Electronics has introduced the UltraGear 25G590B, a 24.5-inch FHD gaming monitor with a native 1000 Hz refresh rate. The panel is aimed squarely at esports players and is positioned as the first consumer display to bring that refresh rate to a standard 1080p monitor.
<!-- more -->

LG pairs the high refresh rate with an IPS panel, Motion Blur Reduction Pro, low-reflection coating, and AI Scene Optimization and AI Sound features. The company says the monitor will launch in select markets in the second half of 2026, with broader availability to follow.
