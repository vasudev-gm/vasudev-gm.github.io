### Quick Report

Canonical says Ubuntu 26.10 will begin introducing AI functionality as an opt-in layer, with a design bias toward local inference, open source harnesses, and transparent model licensing choices. The company frames the plan as pragmatic integration rather than an AI-first OS pivot, with explicit emphasis on user control and responsible rollout.
<!-- more -->

Canonical\'s approach separates implicit AI enhancements such as speech-to-text and OCR from explicit agentic workflows for automation and troubleshooting. Canonical also points to its confined Snap-based inference path and silicon partnerships as the foundation for delivering useful AI features without forcing cloud dependency or broad default data exposure.
