### Quick Report

Apple has announced AirPods Max 2, bringing its H2 chip to the over ear lineup with a claimed jump of up to 1.5x more effective active noise cancellation and a broader set of adaptive audio features. The update also adds 24-bit, 48 kHz lossless playback over USB-C, while keeping the premium positioning at a starting price of USD 549.
<!-- more -->

The new model includes capabilities such as Adaptive Audio, Conversation Awareness, Voice Isolation, camera remote controls, and Live Translation tied to Apple Intelligence workflows. Apple says orders open on March 25 in multiple regions, with early next month availability and color options including midnight, starlight, orange, purple, and blue.
