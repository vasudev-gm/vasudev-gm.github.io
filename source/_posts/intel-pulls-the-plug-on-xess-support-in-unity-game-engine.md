### Quick Report

Intel has discontinued official support for its XeSS Unity integration, with the GitHub project moved into an archived state and no ongoing maintenance commitment from Intel. This change removes a first-party path for Unity developers who were relying on that plugin for XeSS feature integration.
<!-- more -->

Intel\'s notice indicates no further guaranteed updates, fixes, or patch acceptance, effectively pushing long-term continuity to community forks or alternative tooling. In practice, this increases maintenance burden for Unity teams that still want XeSS in active production pipelines, especially as engine versions and rendering paths continue to evolve.
