### Quick Report

Minisforum and Intel have unveiled two all-flash NAS products, the compact S5 and the higher-capacity S7, both built around Intel Core Series 3 platform options. The S5 uses five M.2 PCIe 4.0 slots in a fanless layout, while the S7 pushes to seven NVMe bays for heavier home-lab or prosumer storage workloads.
<!-- more -->

Connectivity is a major focus across both designs, including 10 GbE class networking and dual USB4 40 Gbps ports, with the S5 also featuring Wi-Fi 7 and HDMI 2.1. Minisforum also highlights bundled AI-assisted management features through its MinisOpenClaw stack, although the company has not yet announced pricing or retail availability.
