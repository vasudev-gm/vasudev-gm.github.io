### Quick Report

Arctic has introduced the TP-4, a new premium thermal pad built to move heat efficiently while remaining safe and easy to work with. The company is positioning it for everything from RAM and chipsets to graphics cards, laptops, and gaming consoles.<!-- more -->

The pad comes in 0.5 mm, 1.0 mm, and 1.5 mm thicknesses, can be layered up to 2.0 mm, and is described as non-conductive and non-adhesive. Arctic says it improves thermal resistance versus TP-3 while staying compressible enough to fit uneven surfaces.
