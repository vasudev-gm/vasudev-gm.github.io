### Quick Report

Micron announced shipment of its 245 TB 6600 ION SSD, positioning it as the highest-capacity commercially available SSD and targeting AI, hyperscale, and cloud data-lake deployments. The drive is available in U.2 and E3.L form factors and is built on Micron\'s G9 QLC NAND platform for high-density rack-scale storage.
<!-- more -->

According to Micron figures, the 245 TB configuration materially improves storage density and can reduce rack count versus HDD-based equivalents while lowering power and cooling load at scale. The launch underscores how enterprise storage design is shifting toward fewer, much larger SSDs as operators try to expand capacity within tight energy and space constraints.
