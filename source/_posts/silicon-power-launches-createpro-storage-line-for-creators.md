### Quick Report

Silicon Power has launched its CreatePro series, a creator-focused storage lineup that spans capture, transfer, edit, and backup workflows. The range includes CFexpress and SD cards, a dual-slot card reader, a dual-connector portable SSD, internal SSDs, and NAS drives aimed at content production.
<!-- more -->

The flagship CR20B Pro CFexpress Type B card is VPG400-certified for sustained 400 MB/s writes, while the BS10 reader, CR72X portable SSD, CR75S internal SSD, and CR55N/CR90N NAS SSDs round out the family. Silicon Power says the concept is built around creator profiles and workflow stages rather than raw specs alone, with QNAP validation backing the NAS models.
