### Quick Report

Intel detailed the Arc G3 series design philosophy as a GPU-first chip with an integrated CPU, optimized for handheld gaming form factors. The company used selective disabling of IP blocks and harvested dies to meet aggressive thermal and power targets without relying on U-segment compromises.
<!-- more -->

Design choices included reducing P-core counts, trimming display engines, and limiting Thunderbolt to match handheld TDP and thermals while prioritizing GPU throughput and supply resilience for the portable market.
