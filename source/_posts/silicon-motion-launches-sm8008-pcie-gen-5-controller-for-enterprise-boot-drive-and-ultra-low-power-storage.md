### Quick Report

Silicon Motion announced the SM8008, a PCIe Gen 5 x4 NVMe enterprise SSD controller aimed at boot drive roles and power-sensitive data center storage tiers. The design is presented as a balance of throughput, power, and security requirements for hyperscale deployment where always-on boot media can add meaningful aggregate energy cost.
<!-- more -->

According to the launch specifications, SM8008 targets up to 14 GB per second sequential performance, more than 2.3 million 4K random IOPS, and active power below 5 W. The controller also emphasizes enterprise readiness through OCP NVMe Boot SSD support, NVMe 2.0a compatibility, multiple form factors, and a security stack that includes AES-256, secure boot, and modern compliance-oriented features.
