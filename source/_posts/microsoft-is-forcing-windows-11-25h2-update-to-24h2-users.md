### Quick Report

Microsoft has started force-upgrading eligible Windows 11 24H2 systems to 25H2 in a staged rollout, with restart timing options but no full opt-out path for standard update flows. The transition aligns with lifecycle planning as support emphasis shifts away from older branches.
<!-- more -->

The update strategy appears tied to Microsoft\'s broader platform split, where later feature tracks may diverge by device class while mainstream x86 fleets are consolidated on supported channels. For users and IT admins, the practical change is less about a dramatic feature jump and more about staying on a branch that continues to receive ongoing fixes and support coverage.
