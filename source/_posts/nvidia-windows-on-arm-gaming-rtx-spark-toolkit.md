### Quick Report

NVIDIA has officially released its first preview build of the CUDA toolkit for its RTX Spark platform, enabling native Windows-on-Arm optimization for ecosystem developers and game developers. For decades, PC gaming has focused on x86-based operating systems, but NVIDIA expects this to change as it rolls out optimization toolkits to create native, optimized Arm builds for its RTX Spark mini PCs and laptops.
<!-- more -->

SEGA has already committed to the platform, releasing AAA titles optimized for RTX Spark including VIRTUA FIGHTER CROSSROADS, with more SEGA-branded titles coming. Considering NVIDIA\'s plans for additional RTX Spark iterations with new CPU and GPU microarchitectures, ecosystem adoption will be essential to the platform\'s long-term success and viability.
