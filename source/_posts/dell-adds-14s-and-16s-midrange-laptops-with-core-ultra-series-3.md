### Quick Report

Dell has quietly expanded its lineup with the new 14S and 16S, two midrange productivity laptops positioned below the XPS family. Both systems use Intel Core Ultra Series 3 processors, Copilot+ support, OLED display options, Wi-Fi 7, and a 70 Wh battery with 65 W USB-C charging.
<!-- more -->

The 14S can be configured with up to a Core Ultra 9 386H, 32 GB LPDDR5X, and a 14-inch FHD+ OLED touchscreen, while the higher-end X7 358H option swaps in Intel Arc graphics, a 1 TB SSD, and a touch OLED panel. The 16S stretches that formula into a larger chassis with optional 2.8K OLED touch displays, and pricing starts at $1,319.
