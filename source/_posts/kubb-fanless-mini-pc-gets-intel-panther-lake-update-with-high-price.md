### Quick Report

Bleu Jour has refreshed the Kubb Fanless mini PC with the Intel Core Ultra X7 358H and Arc B390 iGPU, but the new configuration starts at EUR 1,850. That base model includes Windows 11 Pro, 16 GB dual-channel DDR5-5600 memory, and a 1 TB PCIe Gen4 SSD in a 12 x 12 cm passive chassis.
<!-- more -->

Upgrade pricing pushes the system much higher, with options up to 64 GB DDR5-6400 and 4 TB storage taking the configuration to roughly EUR 2,921.90. The machine keeps a broad I/O mix for its size, including Thunderbolt 4, HDMI 2.1, multiple USB 3.2 Gen 2 ports, and dual RJ45 networking in a fully fanless 2.3 kg aluminum design.
