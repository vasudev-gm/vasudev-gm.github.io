### Quick Report

EA has reportedly laid off developers across the four studios associated with Battlefield 6 development, even as the title posted a strong commercial launch and contributed to record quarterly results. Reports indicate the cuts affect Criterion, DICE, Motive, and Ripple Effect, though the exact number of impacted roles has not been publicly confirmed.
<!-- more -->

Company messaging frames the move as a selective organizational realignment based on community priorities and Battlefield Labs feedback. The timing has intensified discussion around live-service staffing volatility, especially following other recent reductions at major publishers and at additional EA teams earlier this year.
