### Quick Report

LG Display has announced mass production of what it describes as the first laptop LCD panel to operate across a 1 Hz to 120 Hz refresh window in production volume. The panel is designed to drop to very low refresh during static workloads and ramp up for motion-heavy content, aiming to reduce power draw without sacrificing smoothness when needed.
<!-- more -->

According to LG Display, the design uses proprietary circuit algorithms and oxide TFT tuning to cut low-refresh leakage, with claimed battery runtime improvements versus existing approaches. Initial deployment is tied to Dell’s premium XPS lineup, and the company says similar low-frequency technology for OLED laptop panels is planned for later generations.
