### Quick Report

MSI IPC has unveiled the MS-CF27, a 3.5-inch single-board computer aimed at fanless and low-power industrial deployments using Intel Alder Lake N, Twin Lake N, and Amston Lake processor options. The board supports up to 32 GB DDR5 via a single SO-DIMM slot and targets edge automation, networking, and embedded gateway workloads.<!-- more -->

The broad I/O for compact installations, including four Intel 2.5GbE ports, multiple USB interfaces, up to six serial COM ports, and triple-display capability through HDMI, DisplayPort, and LVDS or eDP paths. MSI IPC also lists 12 V to 24 V wide-range DC input and M.2 expansion options, positioning the platform for ruggedized control systems, digital signage, and machine-vision-adjacent deployments.
