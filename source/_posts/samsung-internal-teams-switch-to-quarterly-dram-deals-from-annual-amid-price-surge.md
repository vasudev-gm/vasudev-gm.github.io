### Quick Report

Samsung is transitioning from annual DRAM contracts to quarterly deals as memory prices continue to surge. This strategic shift gives Samsung greater flexibility to respond to market volatility and capitalize on rising demand for memory chips.<!-- more -->

The move reflects the current dynamics in the DRAM market, where prices have climbed sharply due to supply constraints and increased demand from AI, data centers, and consumer electronics. By adopting shorter contract cycles, Samsung can adjust pricing more frequently and maximize revenue during periods of strong demand, while also managing risk during potential downturns.
