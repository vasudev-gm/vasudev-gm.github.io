### Quick Report

Silicon Power showcased XPower DDR5 memory (DDR5-6000/7200/8000 with EXPO-ULL profiles) and the XS90 M.2 NVMe Gen5 SSD family with up to 14.3 GB/s reads and capacities up to 4 TB.
<!-- more -->

The XPower Cyclone kits are ASUS ROG certified and feature ARGB diffusers that supports ASUS AuraSync RGB with EXPO-ULL profiles; the XS90 SSDs offer DRAM cache variants with write speeds up to 13.4 GB/s on larger capacities and are targeted at gamers and enthusiasts.
