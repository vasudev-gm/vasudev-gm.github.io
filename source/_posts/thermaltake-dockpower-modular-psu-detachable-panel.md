### Quick Report

Thermaltake showed DockPower, a modular PSU whose connections panel detaches so users can pre-connect cables and then slot the panel back in; at Computex we saw a 1,000 W 80 Plus Gold demo unit.
<!-- more -->

The tool-free detachable panel aims to simplify cable routing in cramped builds. Thermaltake demonstrated the approach on a single 12V-2x6-equipped sample and suggested the concept may spread across its product stack.
