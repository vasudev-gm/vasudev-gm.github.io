### Quick Report

Apple\'s latest iPad Air comes in 11-inch and 13-inch models, powered by the M4 chip for high performance and efficiency. The tablets support Wi-Fi 7, 5G, eSIM, and Apple Intelligence (beta), with FaceTime, Siri, and Swift Playgrounds available.<!-- more -->

From Apple\'s overview, the new iPad Air lineup adds Liquid Retina displays on both sizes, support for Apple Pencil Pro, and a redesigned Magic Keyboard with a 14-key function row and larger trackpad. Apple also highlights a 12MP Center Stage front camera, a 12MP rear camera with 4K video, dual microphones, and landscape stereo speakers for richer calls and media use.

Connectivity and mobility are central to the update, with USB-C, Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6, Thread, and a new C1X cellular modem claimed to deliver up to 50% faster cellular data performance in supported regions. Apple lists four finishes (Space Grey, Blue, Purple, and Starlight) and reiterates all-day battery life with performance gains over earlier M1-based iPad Air models.

The iPad Air is designed with recycled materials and manufactured using low-carbon electricity, with final assembly sites verified as zero waste. Apple\'s environmental section lists 30% recycled material by weight, 40% renewable electricity in manufacturing, and 100% fibre-based packaging. The device also complies with strict chemical restrictions. For full details, see the official Apple overview and specs pages.
