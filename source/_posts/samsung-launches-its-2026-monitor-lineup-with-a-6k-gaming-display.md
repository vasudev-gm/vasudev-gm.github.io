### Quick Report

Samsung has unveiled its 2026 monitor range, led by a 32-inch Odyssey G8 that becomes the first 6K gaming monitor in the industry. The lineup also includes a 27-inch 5K Odyssey G8, new Odyssey OLED models, ViewFinity S8 monitors, and a Movingstyle Essential display.
<!-- more -->

The 6K model reaches 165 Hz, the 5K monitor goes to 180 Hz or 360 Hz in QHD Dual Mode, and the OLED additions include 4K 165 Hz and 4K 240 Hz options. Samsung says prices will start at $899.99, with the ViewFinity S8 adding Thunderbolt 5 and up to 140 W charging on its 40-inch curved model.
