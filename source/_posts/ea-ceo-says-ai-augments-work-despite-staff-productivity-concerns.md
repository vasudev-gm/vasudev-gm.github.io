### Quick Report

EA CEO Andrew Wilson has defended the company\'s broad AI rollout, arguing that automation is mostly augmenting development workflows rather than replacing employees. Speaking at an industry event, Wilson said about 85% of EA\'s QA flow now uses machine-learning-driven checks for repetitive validation tasks.

The stance contrasts with prior employee claims that internal AI tooling had reduced productivity in parts of the pipeline even after long adoption windows.
