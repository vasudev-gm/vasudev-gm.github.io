### Quick Report

Lenovo has announced a new wave of AI focused workstations across mobile and desktop lines, including ThinkPad P14s Gen 7, P16s Gen 5, P1 Gen 9, and ThinkStation P5 Gen 2. The company positions the systems for on device AI workflows, CAD and content creation, with updated Intel and AMD platform options plus NVIDIA RTX PRO Blackwell class graphics across the range.
<!-- more -->

Alongside the workstation refresh, Lenovo also introduced a proof of concept silicon-anode battery rated at 1,000 Wh/L, claiming more than a 10 percent energy density gain versus prior designs and up to 99.9 Whr capacity without increasing footprint. Lenovo says rollout timing for the new workstation models starts in April 2026 and continues through June in select markets.
