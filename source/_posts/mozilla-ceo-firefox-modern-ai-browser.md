### Quick Report

Mozilla has named Anthony Enzor-DeMeo as its new CEO, with a vision to evolve Firefox into a \"modern AI browser.\" The company pledges that AI features will be optional, with clear controls and the ability for users to turn them off.<!-- more -->

The CEO\'s message highlights Mozilla\'s commitment to user agency, privacy, and transparency. Firefox will remain the anchor of a broader ecosystem, with trust and user choice at the core of its future direction. The announcement has sparked debate among users, but Mozilla insists that AI will always be a choice, not a requirement.
