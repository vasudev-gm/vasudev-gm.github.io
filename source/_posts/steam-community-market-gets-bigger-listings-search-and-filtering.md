### Quick Report

Valve is updating the Steam Community Market beta with larger item listings, better search, and more useful filtering. The market already supports items from more than 13,000 games, so the goal is to make browsing and comparing listings less cramped and easier to manage.
<!-- more -->

The refresh adds bigger item pages, more visible item metadata, a wider layout, and richer price graphs that can combine multiple datasets in one view. Valve also added better asset data and more powerful search controls, which should make high-volume item tracking less painful for regular market users.
