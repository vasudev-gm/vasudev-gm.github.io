### Quick Report

Acasis plans to launch the FlowCore series on Kickstarter, bringing what it calls the first 80 Gbps multi-bay Thunderbolt 5 storage system. The lineup is aimed at AI developers, local LLM users, and video creators who need high-capacity storage without giving up per-drive bandwidth.
<!-- more -->

The family includes TB504, TB504 Pro, and TB504 Air models, with the higher-end versions offering multiple M.2 slots, fanless cooling, and up to 80 TB of storage. Acasis says each bay can access nearly the full 80 Gbps pipe, with support for RAID 0, RAID 1, and RAID 10 plus dual 8K display output.
