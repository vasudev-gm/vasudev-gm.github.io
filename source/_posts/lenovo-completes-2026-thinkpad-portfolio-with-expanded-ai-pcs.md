### Quick Report

Lenovo rounded out its 2026 ThinkPad portfolio with the new X13 Gen 7 and refreshed L14 Gen 7 and L16 Gen 3 models. The refreshed lineup leans into AI PC positioning, with Intel Core Ultra Series 3 and AMD Ryzen AI Pro 400 options across the business-focused range.

The X13 Gen 7 stays ultra portable at 0.93 kg while still offering Wi-Fi 7, optional 5G, and a 5MP IR camera, and the L-series adds mainstream deployment features such as up to 64 GB DDR5 memory and 2 TB SSD storage. Lenovo also highlighted repairability, with iFixit scores up to 9 out of 10, plus starting prices of $1,499 for the X13 Gen 7 and $1,439 for the L14 Gen 7 and L16 Gen 3.
