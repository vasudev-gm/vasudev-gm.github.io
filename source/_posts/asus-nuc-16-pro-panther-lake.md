### Quick Report

ASUS has introduced its most powerful NUC device to date, the NUC 16 Pro, featuring Intel\'s latest Panther Lake Core Ultra X9 Series 3 processor and Arc B390 GPU. The compact system delivers 180 TOPS of INT8 AI performance, targeting both developers and gamers with its advanced NPU and iGPU.
<!-- more -->

The NUC 16 Pro supports high-speed LPDDR5X-9600 memory and offers space for two NVMe drives. It can drive up to four 4K displays simultaneously, all within a 144x117x42 mm chassis cooled by a dual-fan solution. Connectivity is robust, with dual Ethernet, Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6.0, and a Toolless 2.0 chassis for easy upgrades. The device is MIL-STD 810H tested for durability and comes with ASUS\'s AI suite, though specific software features are yet to be detailed.
