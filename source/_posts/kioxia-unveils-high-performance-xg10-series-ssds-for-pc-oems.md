### Quick Report

Kioxia introduced the XG10 series, a new PCIe 5.0 client SSD line aimed at PC OEMs that need higher-end storage for premium systems. The drives are meant to replace the XG8 family and push both sequential and random performance higher for modern client workloads.
<!-- more -->

The XG10 tops out at 14,000 MB/s read and 12,000 MB/s write, with random performance reaching up to 2,000K read IOPS and 1,600K write IOPS. Capacities range from 512 GB to 4 TB, and the drives also support TCG Opal-based self-encryption while sampling now for OEM systems that should ship later in 2026.
