### Quick Report

Lenovo says it has completed the acquisition of Phoenix Technologies\' firmware business, including related BIOS intellectual property and engineering expertise from the Dublin-registered operation. The move brings a key platform layer in-house and extends Lenovo\'s direct control over firmware across its PC lineup.
<!-- more -->

According to Lenovo, the deal is meant to accelerate firmware-level innovation, strengthen security posture, and improve vertical integration while trimming long-term costs. The company also highlighted its 20+ year relationship with Phoenix on ThinkPad platforms, and said financial terms were not disclosed after customary closing conditions were met.
