### Quick Report

Colorful has unveiled its iGame RTX 5070 Mini OC and RTX 5060 Ti Mini OC graphics cards, both featuring ultra-compact designs that surpass NVIDIA\'s SFF-Ready specification. These cards are just 18 cm long, 12.3 cm high, and strictly 2 slots thick, making them ideal for small form factor builds.
<!-- more -->

The iGame RTX 5070 Mini OC comes with a factory overclock to 2557 MHz, while the 5060 Ti Mini OC models reach 2632 MHz. Both use a dense aluminum fin-stack heatsink and a single 95 mm fan. The 5070 Mini OC draws power from a 16-pin 12V-2x6 connector, while the 5060 Ti Mini OC uses a single 8-pin PCIe connector. Pricing has not been announced.
