### Quick Report

Valve is reportedly developing a Steam feature that estimates expected game FPS before purchase, using large-scale telemetry from opted-in systems to map hardware combinations against observed performance. The idea is to give buyers a clearer expectation than static minimum and recommended spec lists.
<!-- more -->

If shipped broadly, the estimator could combine CPU, GPU, memory, driver, and settings context to present likely frame-rate ranges at common presets and resolutions. Accuracy will depend on data quality and normalization across caps, sync modes, and diverse configurations, but the approach could still reduce trial-and-refund friction for many buyers.
