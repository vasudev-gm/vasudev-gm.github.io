### Quick Report

Samsung Electronics unveiled Flex Titanium, a new foldable display architecture aimed at the next generation of Galaxy devices. The design combines titanium-alloy film and a titanium plate to improve durability while keeping the display slim and flexible.
<!-- more -->

The company says the technology builds on years of foldable display research and is meant to reduce crease visibility while improving the overall viewing experience. If it becomes part of future Galaxy foldables, it could become another example of how display engineering is now central to premium smartphone differentiation.
