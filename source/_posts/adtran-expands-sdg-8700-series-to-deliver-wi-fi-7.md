### Quick Report

Adtran expanded its SDG 8700 series with new Wi-Fi 7 gateways that span more of the residential and small-business market. The refreshed lineup is meant to help service providers move Wi-Fi 7 from premium bundles into wider deployment tiers.
<!-- more -->

The portfolio now covers dual-band and tri-band models with 2.5GbE and 10GbE connectivity, while Adtran keeps the fleet on its shared SmartOS software stack with Intellifi, TR-369, and Plume support. That combination gives providers a common management path across residential, MDU, and community Wi-Fi use cases.
