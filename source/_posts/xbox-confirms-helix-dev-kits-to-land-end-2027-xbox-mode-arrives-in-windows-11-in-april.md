Microsoft Xbox leadership has outlined a clearer early schedule for Project Helix, stating that alpha development hardware is expected to begin reaching developers toward the end of 2027. The update reinforces a 2027 to 2028 window for next generation transition while keeping long-term backward compatibility as a headline platform commitment.

The same briefing confirmed a dedicated Xbox Mode experience for Windows 11 PCs, described as an evolution of the full screen gaming shell previously tested on handheld hardware. Xbox also reiterated active collaboration with AMD on machine learning driven graphics technology for future devices, including support paths around new DirectX generation capabilities.
