### Quick Report

TechPowerUp reports that Intel showcased a reference Wildcat Lake laptop that mirrors the industrial style of Apple MacBook Neo, including a slim aluminium body and color focused presentation. The system appears aimed at value and mainstream portable designs rather than premium workstation class notebooks.<!-- more -->

The reported platform uses a 6-core configuration with Xe3 graphics and an onboard NPU, while also supporting multiple power profiles from low power fanless operation to higher boost limits. If adopted broadly by OEM partners, the reference design could raise build quality expectations in the entry Windows laptop segment.
