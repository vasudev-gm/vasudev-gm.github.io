### Quick Report

At CES 2026, Good Way introduced the DBF7059 Thunderbolt 5 eGPU Dock, designed to expand laptop graphics capabilities with high-end GPUs. The dock features a PCIe 4.0 x16 slot, Thunderbolt 5 interface with 120 Gbps host-to-GPU bandwidth, and a built-in 700 W power supply supporting up to 600 W GPUs.
<!-- more -->

Targeted at professionals and enterprise users, the dock also includes USB audio, SD Express, 10 Gbps USB-A and USB-C ports, and a Thunderbolt 5 downstream port for daisy-chaining. The open design improves cooling and allows users to monitor their GPU directly, making it a robust solution for demanding workloads.
