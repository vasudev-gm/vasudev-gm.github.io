### Quick Report

Apple announced that Tim Cook will move from CEO to Executive Chairman, while John Ternus will become Apple CEO, with both changes taking effect on September 1, 2026. The company said the transition follows long-term succession planning by the board and is intended to keep leadership continuity.<!-- more -->

Cook is expected to remain engaged on strategic matters and policy engagement as Executive Chairman, while Ternus will also join the board. Apple said Arthur Levinson will become lead independent director on the same date, marking a wider governance reshuffle alongside the CEO handoff.
