### Quick Report

AMD is bundling the Carbice Ice Pad, a vertically aligned carbon nanotube thermal pad, with a relaunch special edition Ryzen 7 5800X3D. The Ice Pad aims to provide consistent, maintenance-free thermal contact without the need for periodic repasting.
<!-- more -->

Carbice claims the Ice Pad avoids common issues like paste drying and graphite delamination, offering reliable thermal conductivity across the life of a system and potentially extending usable lifespans for existing PCs.
