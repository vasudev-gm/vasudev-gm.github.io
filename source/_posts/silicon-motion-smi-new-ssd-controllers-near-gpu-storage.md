### Quick Report

Silicon Motion showcased new SSD controllers at Computex: the DRAMless SM2524XT for Gen5 consumer drives and the SM8466 Gen6 enterprise controller aimed at **near-GPU** storage performance with targets up to ~28 GB/s sequential and multi-million IOPS.
<!-- more -->

SMI\'s demo figures included Gen5 controller reads up to 14 GB/s and a Gen6 roadmap promising up to 28 GB/s and up to 7 million IOPS; controllers are built on TSMC 6 nm and support NVMe 2.x protocols and large-capacity SSDs for computational-storage roles.
