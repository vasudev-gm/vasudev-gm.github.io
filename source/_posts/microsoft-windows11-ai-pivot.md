### Quick Report

Microsoft is pulling back from its "AI everywhere" strategy in Windows 11, pausing forced Copilot and agentic integrations to focus on fixing bugs and improving core features. The company is responding to user criticism and low adoption of AI buttons, aiming to restore stability and trust in the OS.
<!-- more -->

Internal teams are reviewing AI additions in basic apps like Notepad and Paint, and may remove them to prioritize essential functionality. Microsoft is dedicating more resources to address frequent BSODs, bugs, and reliability issues that have plagued recent updates, hoping to win back long-time Windows users.
