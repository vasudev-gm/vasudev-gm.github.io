### Quick Report

Microsoft says some Xbox Wireless and Xbox Design Lab controllers were mistakenly shipped without the usual **bundled AA batteries**, a packaging issue that appears to have affected units since at least December. The company has publicly acknowledged the error and directed impacted customers to a dedicated support flow.
<!-- more -->

Eligible users can submit a service request and, after serial verification, receive a corrective pack that includes rechargeable batteries and a USB-C cable. Early user reports suggest the process can be inconsistent for some serial numbers, but Microsoft\'s stated remedy is a direct fulfillment replacement rather than a store return path.
