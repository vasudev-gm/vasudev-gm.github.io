### Quick Report

Sony has launched 1000X THE COLLEXION as a premium anniversary model for its 1000X headphone family. The new version is meant to blend refined materials, a cleaner design language, and the familiar noise-canceling hardware from the 1000X line.
<!-- more -->

Sony says the headset will ship in Platinum and Black for $649.99, while the WH-1000XM6 also gets a Sandstone colorway. The Collexion adds DSEE Ultimate, 360 Reality Audio Upmix modes, 24-hour battery life, and the same 12-microphone noise-canceling system used by the WH-1000XM6.
