### Quick Report

Intel has previewed Texture Set Neural Compression, an AI-assisted texture pipeline that the company says can reduce texture data by up to 18x while keeping visual degradation relatively low in its test scenarios. The method is positioned as a way to cut install and VRAM pressure as content complexity rises.
<!-- more -->

The presentation describes multiple operating points, including a milder compression mode and a more aggressive profile with greater savings and higher quality tradeoff. Intel also pointed to early performance data on Arc-class graphics with XMX acceleration, with alpha availability expected before wider beta and production rollout stages.
