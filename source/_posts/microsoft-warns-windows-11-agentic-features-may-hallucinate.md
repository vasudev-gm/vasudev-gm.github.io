### Quick Report

Microsoft has issued a warning that Windows 11\'s new AI agentic features may generate inaccurate or hallucinated information. Users are advised to carefully verify AI-generated outputs before relying on them for critical tasks.

The cautionary note comes as Microsoft expands AI capabilities across Windows 11, including AI assistants and automated workflows. Like other large language model-based systems, these features can occasionally produce plausible-sounding but incorrect information. Microsoft\'s transparency about this limitation reflects growing awareness of AI reliability challenges and the need for user vigilance when interacting with AI-powered tools.
