### Quick Report

Valve issued a detailed public response after the New York Attorney General lawsuit targeting loot box mechanics in Counter-Strike 2, Dota 2, and Team Fortress 2. The company said it does not believe current New York gambling laws are being violated and argued that optional cosmetic boxes and item transferability fit established consumer behavior patterns seen in physical collectibles.<!-- more -->

Valve also said it declined a proposed deal because the requested commitments would extend beyond current law and would require invasive user data collection across global accounts. At the same time, Valve stated it will comply with any future law passed through normal legislative process, while continuing account enforcement against third party gambling abuse on Steam related item markets.
